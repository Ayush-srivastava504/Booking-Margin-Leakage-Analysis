# KPI Dictionary – Booking Marketplace

This document defines key metrics used in the dashboard, their formulas, and business meaning.

---

## Revenue Metrics

### 1. Gross Booking Value (GBV)

**Definition:** Total booking revenue before deductions.
**Formula:**
GBV = Base Price × Stay Length + Cleaning Fee + Guest Fee + Payment Fee
**Current:** $13.74M
**Goal:** Increase volume and pricing while protecting margin.

### 2. Platform Fees

**Definition:** Commission charged to hosts.
**Formula:** Base Price × 3%
**Current:** $2.05M (14.9% of GBV)
Represents platform revenue.

### 3. Payment Processing Fees

**Definition:** Card processing charges.
**Formula:** (Base + Guest Fee) × 2%
**Current:** $0.27M (2.0% of GBV)

### 4. Net Payout to Host

**Definition:** Revenue received by host before operating costs.
**Formula:** GBV − Platform Fees − Payment Fees
**Current:** $11.42M

---

## Cost Metrics

### 5. Total Host Costs

Includes:

* Cleaning: 15% of booking value
* Supplies: 5%
* Maintenance: Fixed allocation

**Current:** $2.56M (18.6% of GBV)

### 6. Cleaning Cost %

Cleaning cost ÷ GBV
Largest operational expense.

### 7. Supplies Cost %

Supplies cost ÷ GBV
Secondary, controllable expense.

---

## Profitability Metrics

### 8. Net Host Margin ($)

**Formula:** Net Payout − Host Costs
**Current:** $9.18M total

### 9. Margin %

**Formula:** Net Margin ÷ GBV
**Current:** 34.87%
Target for strong segments: >50%.

### 10. Profitability Status

* Profitable: Margin > 0
* Loss-making: Margin < 0

**Current:**

* 78.7% profitable
* 21.3% loss-making

## Summary

Key Issues:

* 21% loss-making bookings
* 8.5% cancellation rate
* Shared rooms structurally weak
* High margin concentration among top hosts

Key Focus Areas:

* Reduce cancellations
* Optimize pricing by segment
* Scale high-margin geographies
* Protect top-performing hosts

These KPIs form the basis for evaluating profitability, risk, and growth strategy.
